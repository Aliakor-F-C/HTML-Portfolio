About Me

Greetings! I am Aliakor Franklin, a biomedical scientist and an aspiring full-stack web developer who is passionate about creativity and technology. I began learning web development in August 2025, starting with HTML, with plans to progress into CSS, JavaScript, and beyond as I continue to sharpen my skills. My long-term goal is to grow into a well-rounded full-stack developer.

I enjoy being creative and innovative, and for me, technology is the perfect space to express that passion. My journey into web development is fueled by a desire to stay connected with the ever-evolving world of technology while building solutions that are practical, impactful, and future-ready.

I am particularly enthusiastic about solving problems and tackling complex challenges that most people shy away from. I find real satisfaction in breaking down tough problems and seeing ideas polished and brought to life as working solutions. As a biomedical scientist, I have been trained in analytical thinking, problem solving, observation, and experimentation, serving as tools I now apply to technological creativity.

Beyond science and coding, I am also a singer and songwriter with a special interest in Afrobeat music. I graduated with First Class Honours and received the award for Best Graduating Student in the Department of Clinical Chemistry at Nnamdi Azikiwe University.

At the heart of everything I do is a drive to learn, create, and make a lasting impact. This portfolio will continue to grow as I do.
